Discover Porto's soul in Tascas

Cozy, local and full of history . These gems offer more than just food, they share stories, warmth and the heart of Portuguese culture. A place where locals meet and where there is no space for hierachy and social divisions

Food that is culture

Porto's tascas delight with iconic dishes deeply rooted in history. The Francesinha, a hearty sandwich, reflects the city's industrial past. Tripeiros, or tripe stew, pays homage to Porto's resilience during famine. Bacalhau à Brás, a codfish dish, hails from maritime traditions. These culinary gems narrate tales of resilience, maritime prowess, and the spirit of Porto.

Football in the heart

Serving as vibrant arenas where football fervor takes center stage. From passionate debates over iconic matches to the shared cheers and camaraderie, these local establishments embody the heart and soul of Porto's football culture. In every toast and celebration, the pulse of the city's love for the game echoes through the walls of its beloved tascas.

The place to meet

Tascas are are more than eateries, they're communal hubs. Fostering connections, sharing stories, and preserving local traditions. Tthese cozy spots are the heartbeat of community life. In every toast and shared moment, tascas weave a tapestry of unity, enriching the social fabric of Porto. Specially around the working class and neighbourghood loyals.
